Speakers

  • Professor Prathamesh


    Prof. Prathamesh Churi is an Assistant Professor in the Graduate School of Computer Science, NMIMS-Deemed University, Mumbai, and a Research Mentor at Cerebranium, Berlin. A keen research fellow, his interests include Information Security and Privacy, and Technology-enhanced Learning. He has published more than 40 research papers in reputed international journals and conferences. He has received more than 10 honors at the national and international level, and in 2019, was the recipient of the “Young Researcher Award” by GISR Foundation. Prof. Prathamesh has served as Session Chair, TPC, member, convenor, and book reviewer in reputed conferences and journals. He is currently researching the area of ‘privacy of healthcare data’ as a doctoral student in Computer Engineering at Symbiosis International-Deemed University. He is a member of the Board of Study (BoS) of institutes across Mumbai and also a senior member of the IEEE.

  • Mr. Sudhakar Muthumani


    Safety isn’t a luxury, it's a responsibility. It is with this responsibility that Mr. Sudhakar Muthumani founded and presently leads the OWASP Thoothukudi Chapter. While bounty hunting and blog writing, he is right when he calls himself a ‘security person’, given the expertise he has developed over the years. He currently works as a Security Analyst at PrimeFort, Chennai. A programmer who found his passion in security, Mr. Sudhakar has won accolades and appreciation from numerous people, from college administrations to corporations like Xiaomi and zerossl. He has spoken to hundreds at many cybersecurity events, as he works tirelessly to fulfill his mission – to promote awareness even among rural areas.

  • Mr. Sanket Solanki


    A cybersecurity enthusiast with many certifications to his name, Mr. Sanket Solanki is undoubtedly an expert in his craft. He is the Lead Penetration Tester and Project Lead at Accorian, Bangalore. He was in the position of Cyber Security Analyst at Mahindra Special Services Group, Mumbai, before working as Security Consultant at NetSPI and TUV SUD. He is a certified Offensive Security Certified Professional (OSCP), a Certified Red Team Professional (CRTP), a Certified Red Team Expert (CRTE), a Certified Enterprise Security Specialist (PACES) and an eLearnSecurity Certified Penetration Test eXtreme (eCPTX). A hacker and computer geek, Mr. Solanki also routinely blogs on information security-related topics.

  • Mr. Raunak Parmar


    Ever wanted to be someone who’s tried everything? Mr. Raunak Parmar is one such person. Cyber security, writing tools, music production, and even disk jockeying – he’s done it all. A certified Offensive Security Web Expert (OSWE), he is the author of ‘365-Stealer’, a phishing simulation tool used to execute Illicit Consent Grant Attacks. He is currently a Senior Security Engineer at Accorian, Bangalore, before which he was in the position of Security Researcher for a year at Altered Security Solutions. Mr. Raunak, or ‘Trouble1’ as he is known, has a YouTube channel where he uploads Walkthroughs and Guides for the 365-Stealer, and also answers questions about the OSWE certification. A cloud and application security enthusiast, he is also interested in the domain of automation.
